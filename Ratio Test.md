[[Infinite Series]]
## Introduction 
The **Ratio Test** is a convergence test for infinite series that evaluates the limit of the ratio between consecutive terms to determine whether the series converges or diverges.

$$\lim_{n\to\infty}|\frac{a_{n + 1}}{a_n}| = L < 1 => Converge$$
$$\lim_{n\to\infty}|\frac{a_{n + 1}}{a_n}| = L > 1 = \infty => Divergent$$
$$\lim_{n\to\infty}|\frac{a_{n + 1}}{a_n}| = 1 => In conclusive$$
