## How to calculate?
$$ v = \frac{d}{dt}r = \frac{d}{dt}(xi + yj) = \frac{d}{dt}(xi)+\frac{d}{dt}(yj)$$
$$= i\frac{dx}{dt}+ j \frac{dy}{dt} $$
$$ v = v_xi + v_yi $$
$$ v_x = \frac{dx}{dt}, v_y = \frac{dy}{dt}$$
$\Delta r = vt$ Why? 
Because V is constant so v = $\frac{Displacement}{Time}$
